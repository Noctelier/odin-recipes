Smash Burger Recipe

Descrption

This is a fantastic Smash Burger, surely to replace your current burger recipe. Other paragraph text here.

Ingredients
    Ground beef
  • Seasonings of choice(Tony's, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, etc)
  • Buns
  • Toppings (Lettuce, tomato, onions, etc)
  • Butter
Steps
  1. First you want to put your ground beef in a bowl and lightly break it up. Then grab some meat and lightly roll into a ball. Donâ€™t pack the meat in a tight ball, it needs to be loosely formed. I did 4 balls from 1 pound meat. Feel free to adjust burger size.

  2. Turn your Blackstone on high and let it get nice and hot. Or if you are cooking on a cast iron skillet turn to high, add a little oil and let it get hot. Place burger balls on hot griddle. Searing your burgers really well is key.

  3. Then with your burger press, press down firmly to flatten your patties. Sprinkle with seasoning and let cook. We made ours pretty thin.

    The thin texture and hot griddle creates a crispy crust texture on the outside of the burger.

  4. Around 3-5 minutes check your burger to see if it is crispy on the bottom, and if so flip, season again, and let the other side crisp up.

  5. Place buns on griddle to toast. Remove buns once a slight golden color. You can butter your buns or drizzle with light oil on grill to help the buns toast.
